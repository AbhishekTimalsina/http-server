About
Nestled within 10 acres of exquisitely landscaped grounds, HOTEL COMFY is conveniently located 8 km away from the Pokhara International Airport and only 2 km from the Pokhara city centre. We have always been an ideal haven for business and leisure travelers alike, offering resort ambience with an intimate touch. Since many of the distinguished NGO’s and INGO’s in Pokhara are in the vicinity and the UN head office is only a few minutes walking distance. We provide a perfect place for our valued guests to rest, work or socialize.
